📅 11th April 2026 | 🕒 3:00 PM Onwards | 📍 Forma Sports Avenue

General Rules

  • • Max 8 players a side in a match.
  • • All matches will be of 7 overs (Only one bowler can bowl 2 overs).
  • • Only one new soft tennis ball will be provided for each match at the beginning.
  • • Captains to register their team on CricHeros for scoring.
  • • 7 overs must be bowled within 20 mins. After 20 Minutes 1 boundary filder will be removed from ground and after that Penalty of 5 runs per every 2 min delay.
  • • Sportsmanship is mandatory; collaborate with organizers for fair play.
  • • Under any circumstances umpire decision is final and no arguments will be entertained.
  • • Rain policy: Matches continue unless deemed unsafe.
  • • No last-moment replacements; play short if a player is unavailable.
  • • Matches will be played as per the schedule and no changes will be entertained. If any team fails to turn up for the match within 5 minutes of the scheduled time, the match will be awarded to the other team.
  • • Entry fees are non-refundable except reserved players (If they did not play any single match)

Batting Rules

  • • Retired batsmen cannot return to bat in that innings.
  • • Ball touching TOP/SIDE net and caught = NOT OUT.
  • • Ball hitting TOP/SIDE net and hitting boundary = 4 runs.

Bowling Rules

  • • Declare bowling side/changes or it's a No Ball.
  • • Speed gun tracking active: Speed limit breach = No Ball + Free Hit.
  • • International guidelines apply. No Byes or Leg Byes.
  • • Box will be drawn around bowling stumps. Touching any line of the box will be considered as a no ball.

POWEROVER

  • • Power over should be used within first 3 overs from batting team. If not used within first 2 overs, 3rd over will be considered as a power over.
  • • Power over can be used only once in an innings.
  • • Power over should be declared before the start of the over.
  • • Only one filder will be at the boundary (behind the stumpline).

Fielding

Max 3 fielders behind the bowler.
Wicket Keeper is mandatory.
Overthrows are applicable.
